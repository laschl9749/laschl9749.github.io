Bavaria

Bavaria was a duchy of the Franks who had been ruled by the Agilolfings until being deposed by Charlemange and for the next few hundred years would never be ruled by one house for more than three generations, leading to massive loss of land in the south and east especially under Henry the Quarrelsome, which lost as one might presume the March of Austria

Austria

Austria was a small march made on the borders of the Bulgarian Empire along the kingdom of Hungary, made to defend against the Magyars or the Hungarians as they are known, to be a borderland to try and protect against the lightning raids they carried out, going on until the Hungarians in the Carpathian Basin were brought under Christianity and the Pope
